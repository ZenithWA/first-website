This page is dedicated to Pizza


I dont know much about Pizza, except how to eat it. But no worries, we'll figure it out together.
Let's start with a list of things we need to make a pizza.

Shopping List

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Salami

Recipe

  1. Roll the dough
  2. Heat the Oven
  3. Add Tomato paste and topings(excluding cheese) on dough
  4. Throw Pizza in the oven
  5. Wait till the bottom is slightly orange
  6. Add cheese on top
  7. Wait a bit
  8. READY!
